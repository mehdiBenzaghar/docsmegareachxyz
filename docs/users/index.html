Getting Started

Step 1: Create Your Account

Sign up with your email or connect your Web3 wallet to get started. You'll receive a welcome bonus of $REACH tokens upon verification.

Step 2: Connect Your Social Accounts

Link your Twitter/X, Instagram, and other social media accounts to start earning rewards for your engagement.

Step 3: Set Up Your Wallet

Configure your MegaETH wallet to receive and manage your $REACH token rewards.

Step 4: Start Engaging

Begin interacting with content, and our AI will help you create meaningful responses that earn rewards.

Earning $REACH Tokens

There are multiple ways to earn tokens on MegaReach:

  • Daily Engagement: Earn tokens for likes, comments, and shares
  • Quality Content: Get bonus rewards for high-quality responses and posts
  • Referrals: Invite friends and earn when they join and engage
  • Challenges: Participate in weekly challenges for extra rewards
  • Staking: Stake your $REACH tokens for passive income
